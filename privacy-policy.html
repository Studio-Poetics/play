Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Quick Summary: Our Word Sprint games are simple, offline word games. We don't collect personal information, don't require accounts, and don't share your data with third parties. We only use Google Analytics for basic usage statistics.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Word Sprint! This Privacy Policy explains how Studio Poetics ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in our Word Sprint mobile applications, including Word Sprint English, Word Sprint Hindi, and Word Sprint Tamil (collectively, "the Apps" or "our Apps").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be collected through our Apps. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information. Our Apps do not require:

  • User accounts or registration
  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contact list access
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2.2 Usage Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the Apps. This may include:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • General usage patterns (e.g., how often the app is opened)
  • Session duration
  • Crashes and technical errors

This information is anonymous and aggregated – it cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Game Data

Your game progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the app

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous data we collect through Google Analytics is used solely to:

  • Understand how users interact with our Apps
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Make informed decisions about future updates

We do NOT use this information for:

  • Marketing or advertising
  • Selling or sharing data with third parties
  • Tracking individual users
  • Creating user profiles

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Analytics

Our Apps use Google Analytics for Firebase, a service provided by Google Inc. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data as described in Section 2.2.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate with any other third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Data Storage and Security

Since we don't collect personal information, there is minimal data to protect. However:

  • Local Storage: Your game data is stored securely on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to Google's servers
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are suitable for all ages, including children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13
  • No registration or account creation is required
  • No in-app purchases or advertisements
  • No communication features or social sharing
  • The Apps can be used completely offline

Parents can feel confident that our Apps are safe and do not collect information from their children.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Access

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access or request.

7.2 Data Deletion

To delete all local app data:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, or
  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Word Sprint → Storage → Clear Data

7.3 Analytics Opt-Out

While our analytics data is anonymous, you can opt out of analytics tracking by:

  • Disabling "Usage & Diagnostics" in your device settings, or
  • Using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on (for web version, if applicable)

8. International Users

Our Apps are available worldwide. By using our Apps, you consent to the processing of anonymous analytics data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your country of residence.

Anonymous analytics data may be stored on Google's servers, which may be located in various countries including the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through an in-app message (if applicable)
  • Post the updated policy at this URL

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Permissions We Request

Our Apps request minimal Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: Only used for Google Analytics (optional and anonymous)
  • Vibrate: For haptic feedback during gameplay (if enabled)

We do NOT request access to:

  • Camera or photos
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Microphone
  • Phone or SMS
  • Calendar
  • External storage

11. Data Retention

Local Data: Game data is stored on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app.

Analytics Data: Google Analytics retains anonymous usage data according to Google's data retention policies (typically 14-26 months). This data cannot be linked to you personally.

12. No Advertising

Our Apps do NOT contain:

  • Third-party advertisements
  • Tracking pixels or beacons
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Behavioral tracking for ads

We do not monetize user data or show personalized advertisements.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect personal information, these rights do not apply to our Apps.

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process anonymous analytics data based on our legitimate interest in improving the Apps
  • Data Minimization: We collect only minimal, anonymous data
  • Right to Erasure: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • No Personal Data: Since we don't collect personal data, GDPR personal data rights don't apply
